Project timeline

2024 · ERC-2024-AdG

ERC Advanced Grant awarded

GO-Y secures European Research Council funding (Grant Agreement No. 101199575) to build the theoretical foundations of causality-driven graph data management.

1 December 2025

Project officially starts

GO-Y begins its five-year run, hosted by the Database (BD) team at CNRS LIRIS, Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1, with an EU contribution of €2.32M.

2026

Now hiring — PhD & Postdoc

The project opens calls for a doctoral student in Data Management & Causality and a postdoctoral researcher in Temporal Causal Inference in Graph Databases. See open positions →

What If: Causal Analysis with Graph Databases

The foundational result behind GO-Y, published at PVLDB 2025, demonstrates causal “what-if” analysis directly over graph databases — the first step toward causal query operators.
