Project timeline

2024 · ERC-2024-AdG

ERC Advanced Grant awarded

GO-Y secures European Research Council funding (Grant Agreement No. 101199575) to build the theoretical foundations of causality-driven graph data management.

1 December 2025

Project officially starts

GO-Y begins its five-year run, hosted by the Database (BD) team at CNRS LIRIS, Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1, with an EU contribution of €2.32M.

Where the field lives

Community & venues

The conferences, workshops and communities at the intersection of graph data management and causality that GO-Y publishes in and contributes to.

GRADES-NDA @ SIGMODThe flagship joint workshop on graph data management experiences, systems and network data analytics — the most natural home for causal-graph-database work.
LDBC / Graph Data CouncilThe community defining standard graph benchmarks and driving graph schema and query-language work (GQL) — key for benchmarking causal graph workloads.
ACM SIGMOD / PODSThe premier data-management conference and its theory sister, with a long history of causality, provenance and responsibility research.
VLDBTop-tier database conference and home of PVLDB — foundational causality-in-databases and graph papers appear here.
IEEE ICDEA flagship database conference with strong graph-processing and query-explanation tracks.
EDBT / ICDTThe European database systems conference with its theory counterpart — ideal for the foundations of causal graph queries.
CIDRA systems-and-vision venue for bold new directions — a strong fit for early visions of causal reasoning as a first-class database capability.
CLeaRThe Conference on Causal Learning and Reasoning — the principal venue on the causality side of the project.
UAIThe Conference on Uncertainty in AI, with causal inference and probabilistic graphical models at its core.
Causal ML workshops @ NeurIPS / ICML / ICLRRecurring workshops where causal discovery and inference methods meet large-scale data.
TaPP / Provenance WeekThe workshop on the theory and practice of provenance — the technical backbone of database causality.
ISWC / ESWC / The Web ConferenceSemantic-web and knowledge-graph venues, relevant to the linked-data dimension of causal graph databases.
