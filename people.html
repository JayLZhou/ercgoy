The team

People

GO-Y is led from the Database (BD) group at CNRS LIRIS, Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1 — bringing together graph data management and causal inference under one roof.

AB
Principal Investigator

Angela Bonifati

Distinguished Professor of Computer Science · Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1 · Head of the Database (BD) Group, CNRS LIRIS · Senior Member, Institut Universitaire de France · Adjunct Professor, University of Waterloo

Angela Bonifati leads the Database group at CNRS LIRIS and is a Senior Member of the Institut Universitaire de France. Her research spans graph databases, knowledge graphs, data integration, data quality, query processing, and the interplay of data management with machine learning. She co-authored foundational works on property graphs (PG-Schema, PG-Keys) and contributes to the GQL (ISO/IEC 39075) and SQL/PGQ graph query-language standards.

An ACM Fellow (2025) and recipient of the VLDB Women in Database Research Award (2025) and the IEEE TCDE Impact Award (2023). She is Chair of the ACM SIGMOD Executive Committee (2025–2029), a VLDB Endowment Trustee, General Chair of VLDB 2026, and PC Chair of ICDE 2025 and SIGMOD 2022.
